Club Instructions

General Conduct Rules

All members are expected to maintain a high standard of conduct while on club premises. This includes being polite, respectful, and cooperative with other members and staff. Any form of harassment, bullying, or aggressive behavior will not be tolerated.


Safety Measures

Safety is our top priority. Please adhere to the following safety measures:

- Always wear a helmet and appropriate riding gear when riding.
- Follow the instructions of trainers and staff at all times.
- Report any unsafe conditions or incidents immediately to club staff.
- Never approach a horse without the permission of a trainer.


Equipment Usage

Members are responsible for taking care of the club's equipment. Please ensure that all equipment is returned to its proper place after use. If you notice any damage or malfunctioning equipment, report it to the staff immediately.


Stable and Horse Care

Our horses are our most valuable assets. Please follow these guidelines when interacting with horses and stables:

- Do not feed the horses without permission.
- Clean up after your horse in the stables and arenas.
- Avoid loud noises or sudden movements that may startle the horses.
- Treat all horses with care and respect.


Scheduling and Booking

To ensure a smooth experience for all members, please adhere to the following scheduling rules:

- Book your riding sessions in advance through the club's booking system.
- Arrive at least 15 minutes before your scheduled session.
- If you need to cancel or reschedule, please do so at least 24 hours in advance.
- Late arrivals may result in a shortened session.


Contact Information

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our staff at the front desk or call us at (123) 456-7890.
